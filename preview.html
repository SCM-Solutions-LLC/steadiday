Why Families Choose SteadiDay

Safety

“What if they fall or need help?”

The SOS button can quickly call 911, text trusted contacts with location, or call family directly. Safety Sessions can enable fall detection while the app is open, with automatic contact alerts if there is no response.

Medications

“Did they take their medication?”

Gentle reminders and a clear log help make daily medications easier to manage.

Questions?

Frequently asked questions

Is my information private?

Yes. Most data stays on the iPhone and is protected by the phone’s security. Emergency alerts may send location to trusted contacts when needed.

Do I need to create an account?

No. SteadiDay does not require an account or password to get started.

Is SteadiDay really free?

Yes. Every feature is currently included for free during our community phase.

Can my family see my information?

You choose who to add as trusted contacts and what information to share.

What if I’m not tech-savvy?

SteadiDay is designed with large text, large buttons, and simple navigation.

Why is everything free?

We are building SteadiDay with feedback from real families and older adults.

Your data stays yours

SteadiDay was built with privacy in mind. Health information is not sold, shared for advertising, or used to create marketing profiles.
