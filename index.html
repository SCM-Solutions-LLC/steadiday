Why Families Choose SteadiDay

The worries you've already had

SteadiDay was built around the questions that wake adult children up at night.

"What if she falls and needs help?"

The SOS button on her home screen quickly connects to 911, texts you her exact GPS location, or calls you directly. She can also start a Safety Session to enable fall detection while the app is open: if a hard fall is detected and she doesn't respond within 30 seconds, the app automatically texts her trusted contacts with her location.

"Did she take her medication today?"

Gentle reminders on her phone. She sees what she's taken. If she chooses to share a Care Summary, you can see she's doing well — no surveillance, no nagging texts back and forth.

"I don't want to set up another account for her."

You won't. No accounts, no passwords, no subscriptions. Install the app on her iPhone, set your number as the emergency contact, and you're done. Five minutes.

Features

Everything in one simple app

Designed for clarity with large buttons and straightforward navigation. Every feature is free.

Emergency SOS

Quickly call 911, send an SOS text with your location to a trusted contact, or call them directly. Designed to be reachable in the moments that matter most.

Fall Detection

Start a Safety Session from the home screen to enable fall detection while the app is open. If a hard fall is detected, the app prompts a 30-second check-in with options to call 911, text your trusted contacts, or call them. If there's no response within 30 seconds, the app automatically texts all trusted contacts with your name and GPS location.

Trusted Contacts

Keep important people one tap away. Family, doctors, neighbors—whoever you count on.

Medication Reminders

Never miss a dose. Gentle reminders at the right time, with a clear log of what's been taken.

Care Summary

Share a daily summary with family or caregivers — they'll know everything is okay without needing to check in every day.

Daily Tasks

Simple, easy-to-read task lists for daily routines, errands, and appointments. Check items off as you go.

Health & Wellness

Track food, water intake, and sync with Apple Health for steps, heart rate, and sleep — all in one dashboard.

Calendar Sync

Connects with Apple Calendar and Reminders so all appointments, events, and to-dos appear in one convenient place.

Helpful Tools

Built-in magnifier for reading small text, quick access to Find My iPhone, flashlight, and other everyday utilities.

Mind Breaks

Simple, relaxing games and calming activities. A mental break whenever you need to pause and recharge.

Slow Mode

Turn on Slow Mode for easier interactions — larger tap targets, fewer distractions, and more time to read each screen.
