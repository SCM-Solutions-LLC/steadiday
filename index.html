Key Features

What makes SteadiDay different

Safety, health, and daily wellness — purpose-built for adults 50+ and the people who care about them.

Fall Detection

Start a Safety Session and the app monitors for hard falls using the iPhone's sensors. If a fall is detected and there's no response within 30 seconds, all trusted contacts are alerted automatically.

Family Link Premium

Share a private Care Summary with a caregiver — they see medication adherence, check-ins, and activity at a glance. No app needed on their side; it's a simple web link.

Steady Moves Premium

Guided daily balance and strength exercises based on CDC STEADI protocols. Gentle routines you can do from a sturdy chair — with voice guidance and large, clear instructions.

Simple Mode

A streamlined home screen with just the essentials — large buttons, clear labels, and no clutter. Perfect for anyone who finds typical apps overwhelming. Switch between Simple and Standard any time.
