12.1 Emergency SMS Program

SteadiDay™ includes an SMS text messaging feature as part of its Emergency SOS and fall detection functionality. When you enable these features and add trusted contacts, you authorize SteadiDay™ to send SMS text messages to your designated trusted contacts on your behalf during emergency events.

12.7 Safety Sessions & Live Location Sharing

SteadiDay™ includes optional Safety Sessions that allow you to share your real-time location with your designated emergency contacts via a secure, tokenized link. Location sharing begins only when you explicitly start a Safety Session. Your GPS coordinates are held in memory only for the duration of the session and are discarded when the session ends. No location history is built or retained. You can end a Safety Session at any time, which immediately stops all location sharing. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.
